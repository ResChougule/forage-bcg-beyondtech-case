# Installment Financing Research – BeyondTech

**Task Objective:**  
Evaluate the viability of introducing **installment financing** at BeyondTech to enhance competitiveness, align with consumer needs, and optimize revenue.

---

## Key Research Areas

### 1. Consumer Needs & Preferences
- How could installment financing address evolving customer expectations?  
- What drives consumers to choose installment plans? (e.g., affordability, early upgrades, flexibility)  

### 2. Financial Impact
- Have competitors experienced **positive or negative financial results** from installment financing?  
- Could installment financing increase **profitability and customer retention** for BeyondTech?  

### 3. Anticipated Challenges
- What operational or strategic roadblocks have competitors encountered?  
- Could payment plans affect **brand perception, prestige, or customer loyalty**?  

---

## Goal
- Gather insights to determine whether installment financing is a **strategic opportunity for BeyondTech**.  
- Support recommendations with **data, competitor benchmarks, and consumer trends**.
